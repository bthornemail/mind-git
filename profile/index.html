About Me

Research Focus

I am an independent researcher exploring the mathematical foundations of self-evolving computational systems. My work bridges abstract algebra, topology, and artificial intelligence to create new paradigms for computation.

Specializing in topological consensus mechanisms and autonomous AI architectures, I develop mathematical frameworks that enable systems to evolve and adapt in fundamentally new ways.

Mathematical Approach

My research leverages 1,400 years of mathematical development, from Brahmagupta's complex numbers to Adams' dimensional constraints, creating computational systems based on proven mathematical truths.

By implementing complete identity chains and polynomial algebra over finite fields, I build systems that are not only computationally powerful but also mathematically verifiable.

Research Areas

Topological Consensus

Developing consensus mechanisms based on algebraic topology and Hopf fibrations, enabling distributed systems to achieve agreement through mathematical structure rather than computational force.

Autonomous AI

Creating AI systems that can truly evolve and adapt using polynomial algebra and division algebras, moving beyond machine learning to genuine computational evolution.

Mathematical Foundations

Implementing complete mathematical systems from polynomial algebra over F₂ to 32-dimensional trigintaduonion algebras, providing formal verification for all computational operations.

Mathematical Foundation for Self-Evolving Computational Systems

A complete visual programming system with mathematical verification. CanvasL spatial diagrams compile to executable JavaScript with formal proofs, implementing 1,400 years of mathematical development from Brahmagupta to Adams.

Key Features:

  • Complete polynomial algebra over F₂
  • Identity chain implementation (2D→4D→8D→16D→32D)
  • CanvasL visual programming language
  • Formal verification with Coq proofs
  • Production-ready npm package

Technical Stack:

  • TypeScript/JavaScript
  • Coq formal verification
  • Canvas JSON parsing
  • AST generation and code generation
  • Docker containerization
